The Best Movies According to Zelly

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Pursuit of Happiness

It's a beautiful drama about resilience and persistence. About believing in our dreams and chasing after!

The Greatest Showman

I love this musical!! It is a charming, spectacular musical and dance movie with valuable themes about the following: healing conflict, finding reconciliation, showing kindness, discovering and giving real love, unconditional love and more.

Ballerina

Charming movie about chasing your dreams and never giving up!!
